# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Students, currently spanning from class 11 to 12, but expanding down to class 3 and up to higher studies. Their context is active learning, but through an audio-first medium to reduce screen time.

## Product Purpose
NEET Band is an educational platform where students learn by listening to expert-created songs. It also offers summaries, Q&As, and quizzes to reinforce learning. The goal is to make studying interesting and promote eye health by reducing visual strain.

## Brand Personality
Engaging, educational, and health-conscious. The vibe should feel like an interactive study companion rather than a traditional textbook.

## Anti-references
Avoid dense, text-heavy, traditional e-learning patterns that cause eye strain or feel tedious.

## Design Principles
- **Audio-first engagement:** Visuals should support the listening experience, not dominate it.
- **Accessible learning:** Keep navigation and content consumption straightforward and frictionless for all age groups.
- **Eye health focus:** Use colors and contrasts that reduce eye fatigue (e.g., considered dark modes, soft tones).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
No special/complex requirements, but general best practices for eye health (good contrast, legible typography, soft color palettes) are intrinsic to the brand's mission.
